Sudoku

A sudoku generator and solver that I built during a couple of spare weekends in the summer of 2013. Written as an entirely client-side alternative to a very early PHP sudoku solver I wrote many years ago. The project utilizes SASS for a maintainable stylesheet and the module design pattern for javascript maintainability.
